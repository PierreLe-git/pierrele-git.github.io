Why refinance student loans?

Refinancing could help you pay off your student loan debt sooner or bring down your monthly payment amount—all on your terms.

  • You could lower your interest rate with no fees required A competitive fixed or variable student loan refinance rate could help you save thousands.
  • Pay off your loan sooner A shorter term can help you pay off your loan sooner. Plus, you could receive a special rate discount with autopay.
  • Simplify your finances Consolidate all your student loan debt into one easy payment.
  • Free up more cash. You could lower your monthly payments and put more money toward other goals, like buying a home saving for retirement.

How to refinance student loans.

Get prepared

Check the eligibility criteria for info you'll need to apply. And check in on your credit and other financial stats.

Choose your loan terms

See rate options in minutes with no impact to your credit score. Then pick a term and monthly payment that works for you.

Consider a cosigner

If you're not sure you'll qualify on your own, adding a cosigner could help you get approved or secure a better rate.

Complete your application

Finish filling out your info online. This step will include a hard credit pull. On average, approvals take 2-3 business days.
